All About Atlanta

History

Atlanta was formally founded in 1836 during the construction of the transcontinental railroad. It was a large target for the Union during the Civil War and the victim of Sherman's burnings across the south, except churches and hospitals.

In 1886, Coca-Cola began its production within the city.

Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr lived in Atlanta.

In 1996, Atlanta hosted the Olympics.
