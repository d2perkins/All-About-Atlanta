All About Atlanta

Background

The purpose of this document, whether you read it online or on paper, is to give some great tips and things to keep in mind when checking out the city! Whether you’re here for a weekend, or an extended stay, we hope you enjoy the city and all it has to offer! From food, to entertainment, to travel, and more, let this be the springboard that launches you into a great city!

History

Atlanta was formally founded in 1836 during the construction of the transcontinental railroad. It was a large target for the Union during the Civil War and the victim of Sherman’s burnings across the south. Since then, in 1886, Coca-Cola began its production of fine beverages within the city. Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr called Atlanta his home through his life, and in 1996 Atlanta hosted the Olympics. Coming up on 3 centuries old, the city has been through tons and there’s a great deal to learn from the numerous historical spots around the city! Consider visiting the Atlanta History CenterAtlanta History Center in Buckhead for more about the city.

Climate

Atlanta has a tame temperate climate throughout the year. Expect the summers to be warm and the winters to be cold, with random flurries of snow from time to time! Despite being a city in the deep south, Atlanta’s winters can be very mild and cold, so don’t get caught by surprise!
