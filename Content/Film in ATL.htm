Film Industry in the ATL

With the tax-breaks Atlanta provides film companies, the city has seen an amazing amount of interest in filming movies on-location. Works such as Avengers, Baby Driver, The Walking Dead, and more found their place in Atlanta.

The expectation is that the city continues to invest heavily into this growing industry in Atlanta. In doing so, more jobs come to Atlanta which means more prosperity as a whole. Expect lots of movie business to be going on around here! Looking to start your creative career? This is one of the best places to be!
