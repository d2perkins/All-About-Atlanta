Atlanta Entertainment

Mercedes-Benz Stadium

MBS is where the Atlanta Falcons and Atlanta United professional sports teams play. This new, state-of-the-art, facility hosts many events year-round including sold out concerts and college football games. It’s a treat to sit under the massive “Halo board,” a 360 degree circular screen used throughout an event.

SunTrust Park

SunTrust Park is where the Atlanta Braves play. This new park is situated in an open complex that boasts its own dining and shopping options. There’s more to do than just watch a game here! Whether it’s buying Atlanta Braves Apparel or simply just shopping around, there’s a lot to do at SunTrust Park!

World Of Coke

The World of Coke is Coca-Cola’s museum situated in Atlanta. Because Coca-Cola was founded in the city, it claims Atlanta as its home. The World of Coke is that mark on the city which holds an insane amount of obscure soda choices developed by the company! Some say the secret formula is behind a vault situated in the museum.

Georgia Aquarium

The Georgia Aquarium is a family-favorite location, open year-round. “Take a plunge” in the aquarium to see massive viewing rooms to see fish and sea-mammals of all kinds swim freely in massive tanks! For a time, the Georgia Aquarium was the largest aquarium in the world--it holds four whale sharks!

Fox Theater

The Fox Theater is Atlanta’s space for live shows, plays, and musicals. The FOX Theater, with its classic aesthetic, puts on great shows all the time for the whole family to enjoy. Come see a show in the beautiful auditorium, with an amazing ceiling and wonderful stage!
