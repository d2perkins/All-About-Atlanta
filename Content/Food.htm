Food

Because Atlanta is such a diverse city, there’s all types of food for visitors to try out. From classic American to delicious Korean, there’s something for everybody! Below, you’ll find a top-5 list of the best, cheap eateries in Atlanta per TripAdvisor!

#5 - Grindhouse Killer Burgers

Hours: 11:00 AM - 10:00 PM || grindhouseburgers.com

It’s in the name! They serve killer burgers, juicy and stacked tall! However you want it, they can do it.

#4 - Atlanta Breakfast Club

Hours: 6:30 AM - 3:00 PM || atlantabreakfast.com

This southern breakfast joint is a city classic. They serve a delicious chicken and waffles. Be careful though, they only operate around breakfast time, don’t expect any pancakes at midnight here!

#3 - Home Grown

Hours: 7:00 AM - 3:00 PM || homegrownga.com

Home Grown focuses on that delicious southern food. The atmosphere of the place is busy, but you best believe you’re gonna get served by an amazing staff! Enjoy your delicious southern food at Home Grown.

#2 - Thumbs Up Diner

Hours: 7:00 AM - 3:00 PM M-F & 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM Sat. - Sun. ||thumbsupdiner.com

Thumbs Up Diner is a classic American cafe location. As the name implies, you’ll have a great time eating at the great diner! The only review you’ll give is two-thumbs up!

#1 - Aviva by Kameel

Hours: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM ||avivabykameel.com

This Mediterranean restaurant is a cheap, delicious, way to change up your food palate. With light and colorful flavors that explode in your mouth you surely cannot go wrong with a taste of this Lebanese cuisine!
